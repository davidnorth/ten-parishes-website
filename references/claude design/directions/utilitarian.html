Artists / Wiveliscombe / Chris Howe
Artist 014 Wiveliscombe Open 3 days

Chris Howe

  • Watercolour
  • Drawing
  • Illustration

Chris works mainly in watercolours and uses a limited palette to produce paintings which appear fresh and vibrant. His main inspiration comes from the countryside around his home on Exmoor. Originals, prints and cards are on show. Chris also holds watercolour classes in various venues across Somerset.

Visit chrishowewatercolours.co.uk
