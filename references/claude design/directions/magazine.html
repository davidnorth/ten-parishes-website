In this studio Originals, prints and cards. Watercolour palettes set out on the bench. Coffee on, kettle warm.
Also teaches Watercolour classes in venues across Somerset — full schedule on the artist's website.

Chris works mainly in watercolours and uses a limited palette to produce paintings which appear fresh and vibrant.

His main inspiration comes from the countryside around his home on Exmoor — the wooded combes, river valleys, and high moor light that defines this stretch of West Somerset. Originals, prints and cards are on show throughout the festival.

Chris also holds watercolour classes in various venues across Somerset; full details are found on his website. Visitors are welcome to ask about the technique behind any piece on the wall — there's always a half-finished work on the bench to point at.
